About Us

Sho Sho Foundation is a (NGO ) charity organization that try to help people as much as we possibly can with our facilities. We aim to improve social well-being and philanthropy and are also committed to helping children and their families who are in need. We are transparent and open in relationships,striving to meet the highest ethical standards, treating all people with dignity and respect. We also ensure that everyone has the opportunity to live their full potentials by instilling discipline, biblical teachings and moral values.

Our History

Founded with a mission to serve, Sho Sho Foundation has grown from a small initiative into a beacon of hope for many.

How It All Started

It all began with a simple idea: to create a positive impact by bringing people together for a common cause.

Our Mission

The SHO SHO Helping Hands Foundation is dedicated to nurturing the spiritual, emotional, and physical well-being of children in orphanages. We provide for their basic needs and offer financial support to ensure every child has access to education.

Additionally, we provide life skills training and personal development opportunities. While supporting their practical needs, we also guide them in developing a personal relationship with God, helping them experience the joy and comfort of faith.
